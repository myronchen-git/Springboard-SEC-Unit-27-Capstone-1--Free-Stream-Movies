Dark Blue (#1F3A93)
This color evokes a sense of professionalism and trustworthiness. It also resembles the darkened atmosphere of a movie theater, creating an immersive feel.

Vibrant Red (#FF4C4C)
A bold and energetic color that grabs attention, perfect for call-to-action buttons or highlighting important content. Red is also commonly associated with the movie industry (e.g., red carpets).

Soft Gray (#E0E0E0)
A neutral and calming color that balances the vibrancy of the red and the depth of the blue. It can be used for backgrounds, borders, or less prominent text.
